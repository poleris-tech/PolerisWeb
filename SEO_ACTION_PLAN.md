## Common SEO Mistakes to Avoid ‚ùå

- **Don't buy backlinks** (Google penalizes this)
- **Don't keyword stuff** (overuse keywords looks spammy)
- **Don't duplicate content** across pages
- **Don't hide text in images** (Google can't read it)
- **Don't neglect mobile optimization** (you have this ‚úÖ)
- **Don't use cloaking** (showing different content to Google vs users)
